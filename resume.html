New Hope For Cambodian Children

I most recently worked as a 6th grade teacher at New Hope for Cambodian children international school for 3 years, an NGO based in rural Cambodia. The school was created to support by John and Kathy Tucker, a couple from Texas who visited the country over 25 years ago and saw a need to make a safe haven/learning environment for orphans who are HIV positive.
